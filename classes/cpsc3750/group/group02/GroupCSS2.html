What is Dead by Daylight?

Dead by Daylight is a multiplayer horror game where one player takes on the role of the killer and four other players take on the role of survivors. The killer's goal is to sacrifice the survivors to the Entity, while the survivors' goal is to escape the trial. The game features a variety of killers, survivors, and perks to choose from.

Killers

In Dead by Daylight, the Killer role is pivotal, offering a unique and thrilling gameplay experience focused on hunting down and eliminating survivors. As the Killer, your primary objective is to prevent survivors from escaping the trial by capturing and sacrificing them to the Entity. Favorite Killer Perks

Perk Effect
Deadlock You induce mental suffering by crushing any hope of escape. Whenever a Generator is completed, The Entity blocks the Generator with the most progression for 15/20/25 seconds.
Hex: Ruin If a Generator isn't being worked on, that Generator starts to regress at 50/75/100% of the normal regression speed.
Merciless Storm Whenever a Generator is repaired to 90 %, Survivors repairing it are faced with continuous Skill Checks. If they miss one or stop repairing, the Generator becomes blocked for 16/18/20 seconds.
Rapid Brutality Successfully hitting a Survivor with a Basic Attack grants you a +5 % Haste Status Effect for 8/9/10 seconds.
